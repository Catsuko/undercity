# Ashwarden Quarter — Block Reference

Block layout of the Ashwarden Quarter district. A dense working district of tanners, joiners, and small traders, centred on Ashwarden Square and bounded by two graveyards. Guild halls, inns, and garrison posts are scattered through its residential streets.
